About Eulogia Group

Transportatin,warehousing,Integrated Operations

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, end-to-end logistics solutions — from freight forwarding and customs clearance to warehousing and final delivery — enabling seamless supply chain operations for both B2B and B2C customers.

Who We Are
Founded in 2015, Eulogia Group is a global logistics provider headquartered in Los Angeles, with branch offices in Shanghai, Shenzhen, and Fuzhou.
We specialize in comprehensive, end-to-end supply chain services — from international freight forwarding and customs clearance to warehousing, transportation, and last-mile delivery — supporting both B2B and B2C operations.
Our one-stop logistics solutions are powered by an expert team, advanced facilities, and a robust global network.
Eulogia Group operates through three core business divisions:
- Ocean Freight Logistics
- Warehousing Services
- Transportation & Distribution
